What is bmi?

Body mass index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on height and weight that applies to adult men and women. Calculating your BMI can give you a basic understanding of where your health is at.

The BMI breakdowns are:
Underweight = Below 18.5
Normal weight = 18.5–24.9
Overweight = 25–29.9
Obesity = BMI of 30 or greater
