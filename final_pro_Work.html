What and how much should I do?

To start out it is a good idea to get a 1 rep max so you know how much weigt to lift for the main lifts, bench squat, deadlift, etc. For lifts like curls, or lateral raises, find a weight that you can get at least 6 reps with. If you can easily do 8 get more weight. once you find that for a while do 8 Reps at 70% 6 Reps at 75% 5 Reps at 80% and 4 Reps at 85%. After you have done that for a while, or it gets easy do 5 reps at 80% 4 reps at 85% 3 reps at 90% and 2 reps at 95%. After you do that for a while re-max and start from the beggining again.

If you need something to calculate your percentages, visit the calculator page. Below is an example workout. It is simple and can be good for anyone. However everyone is different and it mmay not work as good for some. This is just an example. You can create your own workout from the list below if you wish!

Example Workout

5 Days a week, MWF Chest Shoulders, Triceps, TT Legs, Back, Biceps

MWF

Chest: Bench Press, Incline Dumbell Bench, Decline Bench

Shoulders: Shoulder Press, Lateral Raises, Side Lateral Raises

Triceps: Tricep Pull Downs, Dips, Skull Crushers

TT

Legs: Squats, Deadlifts, Leg Press

Back: Lat Pulldown, Rows, Pull Ups

Biceps: Chin Ups, Dumbell Curls, Hammer Curls
